A Few Words About House

Avatar luxury bungalows

Avatar-inspired by the unconventional style of architecture got featured in the India Today magazine as the first eco-friendly house in Mumbai. Spread across 13,500 sqft and surrounded by old banyan trees, the bungalow sits in perfect harmony with nature. Situated in a peaceful and serene locale within Mumbai city, it is impeccably maintained and easily accessible by all means of transport. Located in Madh Island, the bungalow is ideal for events, product launches, film/serial shoots, photoshoots, corporate meetups, and corporate training exercises.

Property Highlights

Electrical capacity

● Adani Electricity provides electricity and, the amount payable is as per the actual usage.
● The bungalow only provides a service light and is included in the daily license fee.
● A generator is not present as most units bring them along.

Loading and Storage

● There is ample space in the bungalow premise for 2-3 mid-size vanity cars and a generator.
● Loading and unloading of props can take place in a fuss-free manner.

Parking and Disposal

The bungalow premise can accommodate at least 8-10 cars. There is also an adjoining plot for additional parking space that can accommodate up to 25-30 cars.



A Few Words About House

Luxury bungalows

Only 30 minutes from the city of Mumbai, Avatar bungalow is a resort-like house specially designed as a commercial production facility, the only one of its kind. Avatar bungalow is maintained and fully prepped with a land area of 13,500 sqft and French size windows approx. 600sqft in kitchen, rooms, and bathroom. Furniture, lamps, ornaments, rugs, and all other props and accessories can be relocated from room to room as per your creative needs.
